    How Long A Cake Last In The Fridge?
    From a Christmas dinner to a house-warming party, delicious servings of baked goods are always a cherry on the top of celebrations. But when all is said and done, you want to ensure the leftovers are preserved properly. When it comes to cakes, it is always a safe bet to put them in a refrigerator.
    If you want to make sure how long can a cake last in the fridge, you are in the right place. Simply put, the period of these confectionaries remaining fresh in a fridge depends on the type of cake you’re storing. In general, these foods remain consumable for around 2-4 days after you have baked them. On the other hand, store-bought cakes can last up to a week or two in a refrigerator.
    Just make sure you use plastic wrap and tin foil to cover and seal them carefully. This way, these oven-made delicacies can retain their taste and texture in the cold atmosphere.
